The 'Ingels Scape' exhibition invites you to discover the work of a pioneering architect, a master in the art of transforming bold ideas into real spaces. Bjarke Ingels, founder of the Bjarke Ingels Group (BIG), defies conventions to create iconic buildings that transcend the boundaries of design.

This immersive exhibition highlights a carefully curated selection of Bjarke Ingels' most notable projects, offering a fascinating dive into his creative process. Explore iconic structures such as the '8 House,' reimagining communal living; the 'Danish Maritime Museum,' merging modern architecture with history; or 'Amager Bakke,' which combines sustainability with innovative design.

Each presented achievement reflects Ingels' visionary spirit, his ability to uniquely marry form and function, and his commitment to environmental sustainability.
