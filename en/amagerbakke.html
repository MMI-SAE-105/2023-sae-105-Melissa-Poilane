Amager Bakke

As a symbol of ecological progress in Copenhagen, Amager Bakke combines industrial efficiency with environmental sustainability.

Innovative Architecture in Service of the Environment

Amager Bakke stands out with its bold design. Overlooking the city, this structure seamlessly combines a waste-to-energy plant with leisure elements, such as an artificial ski slope installed on its roof. This fusion of industrial activities and entertainment makes it a remarkable example of multifunctional use of urban space.

A Visionary Approach to Sustainability

What sets Amager Bakke apart is its commitment to sustainability. By utilizing cutting-edge technologies, the plant converts waste into clean energy and provides heat to thousands of households. Furthermore, it reduces harmful emissions, showcasing a strong dedication to the environment.

An Innovative Tourist Attraction

Copenhill, with its rooftop ski slope, has become a sought-after destination for locals and tourists alike. This innovative initiative serves to raise public awareness about sustainability while offering a unique experience that combines leisure with environmental education
