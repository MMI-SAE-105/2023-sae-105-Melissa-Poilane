Danish Maritime Museum

The Danish Maritime Museum goes beyond the traditional museum by offering an interactive interactive experience to explore Denmark's maritime past from the water's edge.

The Museum and its Architecture

Housed in a singular building designed by architect Bjarke Ingels, the Danish Maritime Museum blends harmoniously with its surroundings. Its innovative design, partially submerged in water, embodies both maritime history and a contemporary architectural vision.

Maritime Time Travel

Inside, visitors are invited to explore various exhibits tracing the evolution of the Danish navy, from Viking ships to modern vessels. Historical artifacts, model ships and interactive and interactive simulations offer an authentic immersion in the maritime world.
